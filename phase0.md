WIN #1: SEED & REACH (Month 1-3)
Goal: Find content formats that get attention and build initial audience.
What we build:
* 50-100 pieces published
* 1-2 hero formats proven
* 5K-10K followers on primary platform
* Repeatable weekly system
Metrics:
* 5K-10K followers (Instagram Reels)
* 10K avg views per post
* 5-8% engagement rate
* Consistent 5-7 posts/week
Platform strategy:1 hero platform (Instagram Reels) + 1 support (Carousels). Focus = no split attention.
Content strategy (70/20/10):
* 70% → Formats proven in similar niches (adapted to your brand)
* 20% → Optimizing the 70%
* 10% → Moonshots (new experiments)
Why this matters:Without proving one format works, you'll waste money scaling wrong content to more platforms.
Unlocks Win #2: Proven format + initial audience + data on what resonates
